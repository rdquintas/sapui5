Experimental Flag

Explains the use of the experimental flag in SAPUI5 and lists the experimental features that are currently delivered in SAPUI5

Some features or controls delivered with this SAPUI5 version are flagged as “experimental”. These experimental features and controls are not part of the released scope of the delivered SAPUI5 version. Experimental features or controls can be changed, or deleted, at any time without notice and without a formal deprecation process. Also, these features may be incompatible to changes in interfaces and functions provided in an upgrade and, thus, break sample applications using the experimental features or controls.

The purpose of including experimental features and controls in a public release is to collect feedback from partners and customers at an early stage, thus allowing the partners and customers to influence the future product with their feedback. Experimental features are not intended for productive use: You must not demonstrate, test, examine, evaluate or otherwise use experimental features or controls in a productive environment, or with data that has not been sufficiently backed up.

To provide feedback, use the SCN Developer Center on http://scn.sap.com/community/developer-center/front-end. By providing your feedback, you accept that Intellectual Property rights of the contributions or derivative works shall remain the exclusive property of SAP.
