Resource Handling

Introduces the concept of resource handling in SAPUI5

The handling of resources in SAPUI5 is divided in a client-side and a server-side part. The two parts are complementary and do not depend on each other.

The server-side mechanism is an optional component to improve the client-server interaction (server-side locale fallback instead of client-side with multiple requests). The server-side resource handling is mainly used in Eclipse to support the modularized development of SAPUI5 applications and libraries.
